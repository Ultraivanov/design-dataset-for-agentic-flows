# Contributing

This dataset grows through people with taste pointing at sites they consider exemplary in their field.

## What we need from you

A link. That's it.

Optionally: one or two sentences on why you think the site is exemplary, and the name you'd like credited on the entry.

We write the structured analysis. You don't touch YAML.

## How to submit

**Easiest way — open an issue:**

[Suggest a site →](https://github.com/Ultraivanov/design-dataset-for-agentic-flows/issues/new?template=suggest-a-site.yml)

GitHub will walk you through a short form. Takes under a minute.

**If you prefer:** DM the maintainer directly with a link.

## What makes a good submission

- A site whose design you'd hold up as a reference in a conversation with peers.
- Ideally within a category you work in or know well from the inside.
- Preferably still live; archived sites are accepted but less useful.

We don't accept:

- Your own client work submitted without context (if you made it, say so — it's fine, just be upfront).
- Template-based sites with no distinctive point of view.
- Sites where the visible design is mostly stock components and a logo.

## What happens after you submit

1. The maintainer reviews the link within a few days.
2. If accepted, the maintainer writes a full structured analysis in YAML.
3. Your name (and optional handle/site) are credited in the entry under `credits.suggested_by`.
4. The entry is merged into `main`, the issue is closed and linked to the commit.

If the site isn't a fit, we'll say so in the issue with a brief reason. No hard feelings either way.

## Why this exists

See the [README](./README.md) for the dataset's purpose. Short version: this is reference material for AI tools that generate or critique UI, and a way to make good taste legible to machines.
